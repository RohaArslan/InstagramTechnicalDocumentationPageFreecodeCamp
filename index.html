Account Creation

Creating an Instagram account is easy. Follow our step-by-step guide on setting up your profile, choosing a username, and connecting with friends.

To create an Instagram account, download the Instagram app from your app store, or visit the website.

1. Open the Instagram app or website.

2. Click "Sign Up" or "Create Account."

3. Enter your email address and create a username and password.

4. Complete your profile information and add a profile picture.

5. Start connecting with friends by searching for their usernames or importing contacts.

Once your account is created, you can start sharing your favorite moments with the Instagram community.

Privacy

Protect your privacy on Instagram with our tips and settings. Learn how to control your visibility, manage followers, and secure your account.

Your privacy is important, and Instagram provides settings to customize who can see your content and interact with you.

1. Go to your profile.

2. Click the three lines in the top right corner.

3. Go to "Settings" > "Privacy" > "Account Privacy."

4. Choose who can see your posts, stories, and more.

5. Manage your followers and block unwanted accounts.

By adjusting your privacy settings, you can enjoy Instagram while maintaining control over your online presence.
